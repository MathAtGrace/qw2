Theorem 3.1.9. Binomial Theorem.

The \(n\)th row of Pascalâ€™s triangle gives the coefficients of the expansion of \((x+y)^n\text{.}\) That is, for any positive integer \(n\text{,}\)
\begin{equation*} (x+y)^n = \binom{n}{0}x^n + \binom{n}{1}x^{n-1}y + \cdots + \binom{n}{n-1}xy^{n-1} + \binom{n}{n}y^n.\text{,} \end{equation*}
